The Lovepop Story

Cards that people actually keep forever

Lovepop isn't a greeting card company. It's a magical moment company. Our 3D paper art cards stop people in their tracks—the kind of gift that sits on a desk for years because nobody can bring themselves to throw it away.

We built a whole system for having a stash of cards ready for every relationship and occasion. The right card at the right moment is one of the most powerful things a person can do—and we want ambassadors who believe that.

If you find yourself giving Lovepop cards and watching people's reactions change, you're one of us. Let's do it together.
